Welcome to Rock, Paper, Scissors Game!

Objective:

Beat the computer by choosing the winning move in a classic game of Rock, Paper, Scissors.

How to Play:

  • Look at the screen where you'll see three buttons: Rock, Paper, and Scissors. Your goal is to pick a move that will defeat the move chosen by the computer.
    • Here's how the moves interact: Rock crushes Scissors. Scissors cut Paper. Paper covers Rock.
  • To make your move, simply click on the corresponding button: Rock, Paper, or Scissors. The computer will randomly choose its move.
  • The winner of the round is determined based on the interactions mentioned above.
Winning the Game: The game consists of multiple rounds. Each round, try to outsmart the computer and score a point by winning. The game continues until you decide to stop or reach a certain number of rounds.
